Creating a mobile-centric site

Mobile-centric sites need to be crisp, informative, and clean.

Notes for creating an A-worthy, polished, and stylish site.

  • Keep the info revelent and easy to access.
  • Try not to use large images in order to properly display on various mobile devices and for a quick load of the page.
  • Keep it as simple as possible, most users who visit mobile sites are looking for specific information.
