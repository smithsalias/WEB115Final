Creating a mobile-centric site...

Mobile-centric sites need to be crisp, informative, and clean. Users need to be able to access the page in a timely manner without a lot of redundant material.

Tips to create an A-worthy, polished, and stylish site...

  • Keep the info revelent, readable, and easy to access.
  • Try not to use large images in order to properly display on various mobile devices and for a quick load of the page.
  • Keep it as simple as possible, most users who visit mobile sites are looking for specific information.
